Your profile

In early-stage companies, commercialization rarely advances uniformly. A company may have strong technology and still be held back by product definition, customer evidence, team capacity, market access, or manufacturing readiness.

This chart shows where your company has built strength and where the next constraint is likely to emerge. Use it to focus your roadmap and commercialization plan. Not every area needs to advance at the same pace, but understanding where you stand is essential to making sound allocation decisions.

The full Spark assessment evaluates technology readiness across nine levels and commercialization readiness through a gated model that includes regulatory, certification, and qualification requirements. Spark uses the model in the Advanced Energy Accelerator and with SparkSphere alumni companies.
